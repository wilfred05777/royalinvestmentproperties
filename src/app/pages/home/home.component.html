HOW IT WORKS

We pay cash for your rural real estate, and make the process as simple as possible.
- We will agree on a cash price for your property
- We send a notary to your home, office, or preferred location near you with the final documentation for your signature and a Cashier’s check (as good as cash) in the amount of the price we have agreed upon
Once the final documentation has been signed, the notary will provide you with the Cashier’s Check
You cash the Cashier’s Check
We save land owners money by handling all of the document preparation and county filing it takes to transfer ownership.

In some of cases, we will purchase the property through escrow and obtain title insurance. If this is the situation, the process is the same but may take a few weeks longer. Please email us with any questions you may have regarding the transaction process.
